1 1/3 lb ground turkey
1 1/2 c chunky salsa
10 oz whole kernel corn, drained
3/4 c mayo or sour cream
1 t chili powder
2 c tortilla chips, crushed
2 c shredded Mexican cheese

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Brown turkey in a large skillet, stirring to crumble.  Remove from heat, drain liquid.  Stir in salsa, corn, mayo, and chili powder.

In 2 quart casserole dish, layer the turkey mixture, tortilla chips, and cheese twice, ending with cheese on top.

Bake for 20 minutes uncovered in the preheated oven, until cheese is melted and the dish is thoroughly heated.
